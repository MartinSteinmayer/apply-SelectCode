ChatGPT's Chocolate Chip AI Cookies

The perfect cookie recipe, generated by AI, for humans!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar.
  3. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients into the wet mixture. Mix until just combined.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls onto a baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  9. Cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
